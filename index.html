I believe the right environment is essential for teams to deliver their best work. With over 20 years of experience in photography, copywriting, graphic design, art direction, and creative direction, I bring a diverse perspective to my role. I've had the privilege of working with exceptional agency and in-house teams across the entertainment, healthcare, manufacturing, and publishing industries. Currently, I combine creative direction and product management for the Universal Parks and Destinations Global Design System, collaborating with teams in North America and Asia.

Creativity

Creativity involves embracing discomfort, exploring familiar paths, and daring to venture into new territories. Our best work emerges when we deeply understand the core issues and the people affected by them.

Process

A reliable, repeatable process is crucial for consistent results. It’s about having just enough structure to ensure key points are addressed, without stifling innovation and breakthrough work. The User Centered Design process, combined with agile project management, minimizes overhead and keeps the team focused on solving real issues.

Leadership

Effective leadership provides predictability and integrity, even in an industry defined by disruption. Fear and stress diminish creative capacity, so I strive to be clear, candid, and supportive of my team’s professional development. I employ a blend of Situational Leadership and Servant Leadership to help individuals and teams excel, meeting people where they are and co-creating a path forward. These principles also apply to influential leadership. By understanding individuals deeply, I achieve my goals in a way that aligns with their aspirations (inspired by Outward Mindset).
